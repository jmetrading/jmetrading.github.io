Introduction

JME E AND I TRADING, AUTOMATION AND TELECOM WORKS, came into existence in JUNE 2016.

The organization was established with the objective of providing high quality Medical Supplies & Instrumentation, Telecom, Automation (PLC'S), Process Control solutions and a variety of complete instrumentation products and services to South Africa’s rapidly expanding process industries. Our clientele includes Medical Institutions, Health Clinics, Pharmacies, Health Care Centres, Residential, Commercial, Retail, Power, oil and gas, auto motive, mining, manufacturing, FMCG, paper, chemicals, etc.

Our services for customers includes install, maintain, replace, supply, design, detailed engineering, application soft-ware development, project execution and management, integration of hard-ware and software, site installation, commissioning and support, training and after sales support for a variety of Telecom & automation(PLC) systems and a wide range of field, Medical and control room instrumentation products.

We also provide complete solutions including supply and installation of PLC’s, Medical Supplies & Instruments Systems), and Industrial Instrumentation, Telecom and IT products based on specific customer requirements which other instrument vendors normally refuse to tread on.

About Us

  • Vision

    Our Vision is to offer the best Products in Medical Industry & Instruments and Automation & Telecom services to our customers. To always exceed customer expectations resulting in customer delight. Our mission is to provide highest possible quality of Medical Supplies & Instrumentation, Automation (PLC’s) and Telecom Systems at the right price. Our commitment to offer nothing but the very best is reflected in our vision & mission statements. We exist because of our customers and we are very grateful to our customers who have made us what we are today. We now strive to take our organization to the next level so that we can serve our customers even better and continue to keep them fully satisfied.

  • Mission

    The Primary Business Objectives of JME E AND I TRADING Automation (PLC’s), Medical Supplies & Instruments and Telecom Works are: To cost-effectively manage Medical Supplies & Instruments, Telecom, and PLC’s, Industrial Instrumentation, CCTV Cameras, Access Control, Alarm Systems, Networking, Fiber Optics Systems, Industrial, Commercial & Residential IT Projects for our clients. To enhance the reputation of the group as Medical Suppliers & Instruments, Telecom Systems, Automation(PLC), Industrial Instrumentation, Fiber Optics, Networking and IT specialists. To provide Total System Solutions where appropriate. To develop new areas of specialization that involves information systems and technology-based solutions.

  • Benefits

    When it comes to application solutions, instead of multiple vendors, you have one supplier to deal with. You’re also assured of complete customization and rapid implementation by JME E AND I TRADING Automation’s (PLC) and Telecom suite of services, one of the most extensive in the industry. Developed to offer you a total, application specific automation package, not just products. The team comprises of engineers and Technicians who have proven experience on a variety of automation systems including DCS and PLC’s of Siemens, Allen Bradley, Honeywell, Yokogawa, Schneider, ABB and ( Emergency Shutdown Systems ) etc. SCADA systems of Siemens, Wonder ware, I Fix, Adroit, Cytec, etc. Manufacturing Execution System (MES) Cybernetics M.

  • Quality Assurance

    As a committed and responsible organization, management ensure the highest level of workmanship will be at all times maintained by enforcing strict compliance with standards, specifications and specific client requirements. Fully justifying our ISO 9001 listing. The management of JME E AND I TRADING is fully committed to the implementation of this quality system. Control is exercised in a formalized, documented manner in accordance with our high standard for quality control procedures. However, quality can only be assured by suitably trained personnel and to this end no effort is spared in our constant strive to ensure that personnel stay abreast of developments in their field.
